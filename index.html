Table Banking &
Women
Empowerment

**Table Banking** is a community-based micro-financing model where
group members contribute funds to create a shared pool of resources. Members
can then borrow from this pool to finance small businesses, personal needs,
or emergency expenses, repaying with minimal interest. This approach
promotes savings, financial empowerment, and accessible credit within
communities, especially in underserved areas. Simple, collaborative, and
flexible, table banking provides a sustainable way for members to manage
finances, grow their investments, and support each other's financial goals.
